Gold

Talakoob business card printing is another service that can be provided at Zanprintify. Gold-plated business card designs are printed on 300 grams of gloss, and in matte and glossy laminate business cards, the design is first printed and gilded, and then a laminate coating is placed on the print and gold, which multiplies the life of the gilding. But in other business cards, after covering, the business card is gold plated. An important point in the design of gilded business cards is that it is better not to print a design below the area you want to be gilded, and secondly, do not use thin lines for the gilded area.
